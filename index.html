What is Prayer Force USA?

Prayer Force USA is a united prayer network—believers across America taking their place in intercession for our nation.

Identity first: All who participate belong to Prayer Force USA. Standing in the Gap and The Prayer Watch are two expressions of the same shared mission.

Our Foundations

The United States stands apart from other nations in a defining respect. Unlike nations that adopted Christianity after centuries of existence, many of the earliest settlements that eventually became the United States undertook a treacherous voyage to a distant land for the freedom to worship as Christians. These communities were founded with a singular purpose: to live out their faith—making America’s Christian roots not merely a matter of history, but a deliberate and profound choice.

From the Pilgrims in 1620 to the Continental Congress in 1774, American leaders publicly sought God’s guidance and blessing for future generations. Prayer Force USA continues that legacy.

George Washington, Inaugural Address (1789)
“It would be peculiarly improper to omit, in this first official act, my fervent supplications to that Almighty Being…”
Historical Anchors (optional)
11 Nov 1620 — Mayflower Compact
“…for the Glory of God, and advancement of the Christian Faith…”
1630 — John Winthrop aboard the Arbella
“For we must consider that we shall be as a city upon a hill. The eyes of all people are upon us. So that if we shall deal falsely with our God in this work we have undertaken, and so cause Him to withdraw His present help from us, we shall be made a story and a by-word through the world.”
Our Mission

Prayer Force USA exists to intercede before God, the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, for the well-being and longevity of our nation.

What Prayer Force USA Is — and Is Not

We Are

  • Christ-centered
  • Scripture-anchored
  • Focused on prayer (not arguments)
  • Open to people devoted to Jesus across denominations

We Are Not

  • A political party
  • A fundraising scheme
  • An outrage or division movement

Participation is never dependent on financial contribution.

Cost

There is no financial cost. Participation requires only your time—freely given—because you believe prayer matters.

Standing in the Gap

Consistent Prayer for Today’s Needs

Standing in the Gap is the original expression of Prayer Force USA—topical, Scripture-based prayer focused on current national events and pressing issues. The format is fluid. We don’t watch the clock; Prayer Warriors join and depart sessions as their schedules allow.

Note: This expression will continue to develop in structure and rhythm as Prayer Force USA grows.

The Prayer Watch

Weekly, Structured Prayer for Enduring Themes

The Prayer Watch is Prayer Force USA’s weekly rhythm of united prayer—Scripture-centered sessions built around enduring themes such as leadership, families, justice, mercy, and national renewal. This expression is designed for broad participation and steady consistency.

The Prayer Watch is Prayer Force USA’s response to the national invitation to unite in prayer.

Submit a National Prayer

Submit prayers aligned to national needs. Submissions are reviewed privately. If you grant permission, your prayer may be read on-camera at the discretion of Prayer Force USA.

Please avoid highly sensitive personal information. If this is an emergency, contact local emergency services.
