What is Prayer Force USA?

Prayer Force USA is a prayer call-up—a rallying of prayer warriors.

Our country is divided—but our need is clear: we must turn back to God.

Prayer Force USA is a movement to keep the United States ever before the throne of grace, seeking His help and mercy for this nation.

America’s early settlers covenanted together under God, acknowledging their dependence on Him.

Prayer Force USA carries forward this legacy of the Founders, and...

God’s promise still stands:

“If my people, which are called by my name, shall humble themselves, and pray, and seek my face, and turn from their wicked ways; then will I hear from heaven, and will forgive their sin, and will heal their land.”

— 2 Chronicles 7:14 (KJV)

Based on that promise, Prayer Force USA's purpose is

to seek God, align with Him, and move in His direction—together—for this generation and those to come.

It currently consists of two branches:

Standing in the Gap

A near-daily gathering to intercede for our nation.

The Prayer Watch

A weekly a weekly alignment with the White House's America Prays initiative.

Our Foundations

Rooted in history. Carrying the torch.

America was fundamentally Christian in its founding.

Other nations adopted Christianity later.

America’s earliest settlements were founded to live it.

No other nation in history began quite like the United States. Other nations adopted Christianity after centuries of existence.

But many of the earliest settlements that became the United States braved a vast, treacherous ocean to worship Christ freely.

They were founded with one purpose: to live out their faith— making America’s Christian roots not merely historical, but foundational.

American leaders—from the Pilgrims in 1620 to the Continental Congress in 1774 and beyond—publicly sought God’s guidance and blessing. That pattern endured.

Prayer Force USA continues that legacy.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Prayer Force USA?

Prayer Force USA is a united prayer network—believers across America taking their place in steady, Scripture-centered intercession for our nation.

Do I have to choose one branch?

No. You are welcome to participate through Standing in the Gap, The Prayer Watch, or both—according to your schedule and calling.

How do I get session links and updates?

When you choose a branch in the Take Your Place section, you will receive a Welcome Email with session links and next steps. You can also follow our YouTube channel for additional updates.

Is there a cost to participate?

There is no financial cost. Participation requires only your time—freely given—because you believe prayer matters.

Is Prayer Force USA a political movement?

Prayer Force USA is not a political party. We pray about the real conditions of our nation—some of them difficult and contested—while remaining anchored in Scripture rather than partisan conflict.

Why does Prayer Force USA accept financial support?

Prayer Force USA is offered freely to all who wish to participate. Financial support is voluntary and helps sustain the work long term.

Is financial support required to participate?

No. Participation in Prayer Force USA does not require financial contribution. Prayer remains open and accessible to all who wish to stand with us.

How can I support the work?

Those who wish to support Prayer Force USA may do so financially once contribution options are made available. Support may also take the form of faithful engagement, sharing sessions with others, subscribing, and inviting friends or family to join in prayer.
