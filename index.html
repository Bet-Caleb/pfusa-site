Our Foundations

Rooted in history; faithful in practice

The United States stands apart from other nations in a defining respect. Unlike nations that adopted Christianity after centuries of existence, many of the earliest settlements that eventually became the United States undertook a treacherous voyage to a distant land for the freedom to worship as Christians. These communities were founded with a singular purpose: to live out their faith—making America’s Christian roots not merely a matter of history, but a deliberate and profound choice.

From the Pilgrims in 1620 to the Continental Congress in 1774, American leaders publicly sought God’s guidance and blessing for future generations. Prayer Force USA continues that legacy.

Standing in the Gap

Standing to face the moment

  • When the moment demands prayer
  • When leadership requires wisdom
  • When our nation needs clarity

Standing in the Gap is the original expression of Prayer Force USA—topical, Scripture-based prayer focused on current national events and pressing issues. The format is fluid. We don’t watch the clock; Prayer Warriors join and depart sessions as schedules allow.

The Prayer Watch

Weekly, structured prayer for enduring themes

Aligned with America 250’s America Prays initiative.
Visit America Prays  •  Read the President’s Proclamation: “A Year of Celebration and Rededication, 2026”

The Prayer Watch is Prayer Force USA’s weekly rhythm of united prayer—Scripture-centered sessions built around enduring themes such as leadership, families, justice, mercy, and national renewal. This expression is designed for broad participation and steady consistency.

The Ask:

“The Ask: What if one million Americans dedicated one hour a week to praying for our country and our people?”

The Invitation:

“The Invitation: Will you join with at least 10 people to meet each week for one hour to pray for America?”

The Opportunity:

“The Opportunity: As we celebrate 250 years of freedom, this is an opportunity to rededicate ourselves to the principles that gave birth to this land of liberty. As George Washington famously said in his Farewell Address, ‘Of all the dispositions and habits which lead to political prosperity, religion and morality are indispensable supports. In vain would that man claim the tribute of patriotism who should labor to subvert these great pillars of human happiness, these firmest props of the duties of men and citizens.’”

“This dedicated time of prayer is not only for our nation, but for its people because a nation is only as strong as its citizens.”

FAQ (starter)

What is Prayer Force USA, and who is it for?

Prayer Force USA is for believers devoted to Jesus Christ who want steady, Scripture-centered prayer for our nation—without needing to “fit” a denomination or a political club.

What’s the difference between Standing in the Gap and The Prayer Watch?

Standing in the Gap is topical and responsive (most days). The Prayer Watch is a weekly, structured rhythm aligned with America Prays—focused on enduring themes.

How do I participate if I can’t attend live?

You can still participate by praying along later when updates or recordings are posted. The goal is consistency, not perfect attendance.

Is there any cost to join?

No. There is no financial cost. Participation is your time—freely given—because you believe prayer matters.

How is optional support used?

Optional support helps cover basic infrastructure (hosting, broadcast tools, and production needs) so Prayer Force USA can remain stable and accessible.
