Our Foundations

Rooted in history; faithful in practice

The United States stands apart from other nations in a defining respect. Unlike nations that adopted Christianity after centuries of existence, many of the earliest settlements that eventually became the United States undertook a treacherous voyage to a distant land for the freedom to worship as Christians. These communities were founded with a singular purpose: to live out their faith—making America’s Christian roots not merely a matter of history, but a deliberate and profound choice.

From the Pilgrims in 1620 to the Continental Congress in 1774, American leaders publicly sought God’s guidance and blessing for future generations. Prayer Force USA continues that legacy.

The Prayer Watch

Weekly, structured prayer for enduring themes

Aligned with America 250’s America Prays initiative.
Visit America Prays  •  Read the President’s Proclamation: “A Year of Celebration and Rededication, 2026”

As America approaches 250 years of freedom, we join the national call reflected in the America Prays initiative — not as spectators, but as participants.

The Prayer Watch is a weekly call to stand watch in prayer for our nation. Rooted in Scripture and united in purpose, we gather around enduring themes — leadership, families, justice, mercy, and renewal.

The Invitation

Join us each week online — from your home or wherever you are — for one focused hour of united prayer. Together, we will lift the nation before God with clarity and conviction.

The Prayer Watch gathers weekly at a scheduled time announced in advance.

The Hour We Have Been Given

This anniversary is more than celebration. It is a moment to rededicate ourselves to the principles that gave birth to this land of liberty.

As President George Washington warned in his Farewell Address:

“Of all the dispositions and habits which lead to political prosperity, religion and morality are indispensable supports. In vain would that man claim the tribute of patriotism who should labor to subvert these great pillars of human happiness, these firmest props of the duties of men and citizens.”

The strength of a nation rests not only in its institutions, but in the character of its people. This hour of prayer is an act of stewardship — for our generation and for those to come.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Prayer Force USA?

Prayer Force USA is a united prayer network—believers across America taking their place in steady, Scripture-centered intercession for our nation.

Do I have to choose one path?

No. You may participate through Standing in the Gap, The Prayer Watch, or both—according to your schedule and calling.

Is there a cost to participate?

There is no financial cost. Participation requires only your time—freely given—because you believe prayer matters.

Why does Prayer Force USA accept financial support?

Prayer Force USA is offered freely to all who wish to participate. Financial support helps sustain practical costs such as hosting, communication tools, and production so the work can continue steadily and responsibly.

Is financial support required to participate?

No. Participation in Prayer Force USA does not require financial contribution. Prayer remains open and accessible to anyone who wishes to stand with us.

How can I support the work?

Those who wish to support Prayer Force USA may do so financially once contribution options are made available. Support may also take the form of faithful participation, sharing sessions with others, and inviting friends or family to join in prayer.

How do I get session links and updates?

Email us and tell us which path you chose (Standing in the Gap, The Prayer Watch, or both). We’ll route you accordingly. You can also follow our YouTube channel for updates.

Is Prayer Force USA political?

Prayer Force USA is focused on prayer (not arguments). We are not a political party and not a division movement. We pray with a clear conscience before God.
