Our Foundations

Rooted in history; faithful in practice

The United States stands apart from other nations in a defining respect. Unlike nations that adopted Christianity after centuries of existence, many of the earliest settlements that eventually became the United States undertook a treacherous voyage to a distant land for the freedom to worship as Christians. These communities were founded with a singular purpose: to live out their faith—making America’s Christian roots not merely a matter of history, but a deliberate and profound choice.

From the Pilgrims in 1620 to the Continental Congress in 1774, American leaders publicly sought God’s guidance and blessing for future generations. Prayer Force USA continues that legacy.

George Washington, First Inaugural Address (30 Apr 1789)

“It would be peculiarly improper to omit, in this first official act, my fervent supplications to that Almighty Being…”

Our Mission

Why Prayer Force USA exists

Prayer Force USA exists to intercede before God, the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, for the well-being and longevity of our nation.

We Are
  • Christ-centered
  • Scripture-anchored
  • Focused on prayer (not arguments)
  • Open to people devoted to Jesus across denominations
We Are Not
  • A political party
  • A fundraising scheme
  • An outrage or division movement

The Prayer Watch

Weekly, structured prayer for enduring themes

The Prayer Watch—Prayer Force USA’s weekly rhythm of united prayer—Scripture-centered sessions built around enduring themes such as leadership, families, justice, mercy, and national renewal. This expression is designed for broad participation and steady consistency.

The Prayer Watch is Prayer Force USA’s response to the national invitation to unite in prayer.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Prayer Force USA?

Prayer Force USA is a united prayer network—believers across America taking their place in steady, Scripture-centered intercession for our nation.

Do I have to choose one path?

No. You may participate through Standing in the Gap, The Prayer Watch, or both—according to your schedule and calling.

Is there a cost to participate?

There is no financial cost. Participation requires only your time—freely given—because you believe prayer matters.

How do I get session links and updates?

In the Take Your Place section, choose your prayer path — Standing in the Gap, The Prayer Watch, or both. Your Welcome Email will include next steps, session details, and how to engage. You can also follow our YouTube channel for updates.

Is Prayer Force USA a political movement?

Prayer Force USA is focused on prayer, not arguments. We are not a political party and not an intentionally divisive movement. We pray with a clear conscience about a wide variety of issues that affect our country.
